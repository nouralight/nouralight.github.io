Artist Statement

I'm a digital media student and San Jose State and an artist on my free time. I began drawing and taking photography at age 11, then in high school discovered digital drawing and loved it. I am good at photoshop, and illustrator. My work is filled of vibrant colors which is my signiture. I hope to inspire people with my work. Enjoy!


I have been taking classes in web developement at SJSU and West Valley College. I hope to get better at coding and make some cool art with it. Here is my GitHub. GitHub, and supplementing what I learn there with training on Sketch.


I am learning HTML and CSS and hope to get more advanced in the future, so I am applying most of my effort to learning JavaScript at the moment, and advance to algorithms and back-end projects. The goal is for this very basic portfolio site to evolve as I strengthen and grow my skill set.


Although I am also quite comfortable working in Photoshop and Illustrator, I am interested in exploring a career in web development, rather than web design. Here are some links to some digital artists I find useful and inspiring.
